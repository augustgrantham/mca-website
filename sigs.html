Special Interest Groups

Join a Special Interest Group and dive deeper into the tech topics you're most passionate about. Connect with like-minded peers and explore cutting-edge technologies and trends in a collaborative and supportive environment. From interview prep to virtual reality and web development, our SIG offers opportunities to expand your knowledge, build your skills, and advance your career. Don't miss out on this chance to join a community of tech enthusiasts who share your interests and drive your success. Join one of Mizzou Computing Association's Special Interest Groups today!

Interview Prep

Interview Prep is the perfect place for students interested in learning about careers in tech. We will cover technical interviews, behavioral interviews, the Spring and Fall Engineering Career Fairs, landing your first job or internship, resumes, and more. We also may have guest speakers from companies like Garmin, Microsoft, and others talk about their experiences.

Lead by Carter Cox

Tuesdays at 4PM, Room E3510

Virtual Reality

Virtual Reality SIG provides a place for students to experience virtual reality and create virtual reality games. We provide hands-on experience with the Unity game engine and walk you through how to use it no matter your skill level. Additionally, we do not require students to bring their own VR hardware and provide gaming laptops and VR headsets.

Lead by Ethan Johnson

Mondays at 7PM, Lafferre C2205
